LABORATORY FOR MOLECULAR BIOLOGY CUSTOMER SATISFACTION SURVEY FORM

The customer survey for KEMRI Laboratory for Molecular Biology is intended to gather feedback from its customers about their experiences and satisfaction with the laboratory's services. The survey will include questions about the quality of service, the timeliness of results, the accuracy of tests, the professionalism of staff, and the overall experience of working with the laboratory. The survey will also seek suggestions for improvement and areas where the laboratory can enhance its services. The data collected from the survey will be used to identify areas of strength and weakness in the laboratory's operations and to develop strategies to improve customer satisfaction and overall service quality.
